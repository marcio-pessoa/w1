#### Pulleys relationship
Calculating pulleys relantionship:

$$ s = \frac{B . 100}{A} \% $$

Where:
- A: Large pulley = 60T (Rotor Pulley)
- B: Small pulley = 16T (Motor Pulley)
- s: Speed Ratio = ?

$$ s = \frac{16 . 100}{60} \% $$

So:
$$ s = 26.7 \% $$
